We receive artworks from talented people and help them developing their ideas. You are free to print or not with us, it's up to you.

When it comes to graphic design, we do everything related to printed matter (publications, flyers, posters...), visual identity and advertisement.

printing

RISO Printer-Duplicator is a stencil printer designed mainly for high-volume photocopying, duplicating and printing.

We are using a Riso SF 9350 machine.

SMØR Press is an independent and small publishing house based in Trondheim. Founded and run by the artist Carlos A. Correia and the designer Joana Bruno.
