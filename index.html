books studio&print SMØR Press

Founded in 2017, this is a publishing and printing house run by the designer Joana Bruno and the artist Carlos A. Correia. SMØR specializes in riso printing technique to produce art publications, fanzines and other printed matter with the vision of supporting the printed matter as a very special artistic medium and a dissemination tool for artists.

design:

We receive artworks from talented people and help them develop their ideas. You are free to print or not with us, it's up to you. When it comes to graphic design, we do everything related to printed matter (publications, flyers, posters...), visual identity and advertisement.

printing:

RISO Printer-Duplicator is a stencil printer designed mainly for high-volume photocopying, duplicating and printing. We are using a Riso SF 9350 machine. You can print posters, business cards, brochures, publications or any other printed matter. If you think your project suits the Riso technique, you should try our services!

studio visits:

You are welcome to visit us at Svartlamon, Trondheim. Whether you want to discuss a project, see some of our books or simply have a chat about print, just send us an email at smorpress@gmail.com to make an appointment. We’d love to hear from you.
